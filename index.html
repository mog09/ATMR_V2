About ATMR

Built for Aviation.
Driven by Precision.

ATMR was founded with a singular mission: to solve the aviation industry's most critical talent challenges. Whether you're an employer seeking exceptional professionals or a talented candidate ready to elevate your career, we're here to connect the right people with the right opportunities.

Our team combines deep sector expertise with modern recruitment technology to deliver candidates who don't just fill roles — they advance airspace management and ATM operations. From Air Navigation Service Providers and ATM service companies to airspace design firms and aviation consultancies, we specialise in sourcing and placing top-tier talent across the air traffic management ecosystem.

We recruit across all ATM disciplines: air traffic controllers and operational supervisors, ATM engineers and system architects, airspace design specialists, instrument procedure designers, aeronautical information managers, flow management specialists, and safety leaders. Every candidate we place is vetted to the highest industry standards — because excellence in air traffic management is non-negotiable.
